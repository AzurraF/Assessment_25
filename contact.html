How to join

Usually tutor teachers will receive a google form which they would then share with the class to complete. However, if you happen to be among the people that miss out on the initial application then all you have to do is send an email to the one listed below! However, please note that the people who filled out the google form will take first priority and your email will only receive a response if your application is accepted.

If your initial application is accepted:

You will be emailed the date and location for the trial. And a list of the gear you are expected to bring (equipment will be provided)

Please only email if you missed out on applying via the google form.

badmintonclub@aggs.school.nz.
